Privacy Policy

01

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Kita ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you interact with the bot on Discord. By using Kita, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Kita is a gacha-style card collection bot operated by an independent developer. We are committed to collecting only the minimum data necessary to operate the bot and provide you with the best possible experience.

This policy applies to all users who interact with Kita on any Discord server where it is present.

02

Data We Collect

Kita collects the following data as part of normal operation. All data is limited strictly to what is required to run the bot.

🪪

User IDs

Your Discord User ID is stored to identify your account, link it to your card collection, track your pull history, and manage your in-game economy (coins, inventory, pity counters). This is a numeric identifier provided by Discord and contains no personally identifiable information.

🏠

Server IDs

Discord Server (Guild) IDs are stored to maintain per-server configurations, leaderboards, and server-specific gameplay settings. This allows Kita to function correctly across multiple servers without mixing data between communities.

💬

Message Content (command processing only)

Kita uses the Message Content Intent solely to detect and process prefix-based commands (e.g., !pull, !collection). Message content is read transiently in memory to parse commands and is never written to disk, logged, or retained in any form after the command has been processed.

👥

Server Member Data (limited)

Kita uses the Server Members Intent to access basic member information (User IDs and usernames) for features such as leaderboards, trading, and gifting cards to other users. No sensitive member data is stored or processed beyond what is necessary for these features.

03

How We Use Your Data

Data collected by Kita is used exclusively for the following gameplay and operational purposes:

  • Maintaining your card collection, inventory, and pull history
  • Tracking your in-game economy (coins, gems, trade history)
  • Managing pity counters and pull rate tracking
  • Generating server and global leaderboards
  • Processing card trades and gifts between users
  • Enforcing blacklists and abuse prevention measures
  • Storing per-server configuration preferences
04

What We Don't Do

We want to be explicit about practices we do not engage in:

✗

We do not log or store private (DM) conversations

✗

We do not store message content beyond command processing

✗

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties

✗

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing

✗

We do not collect real names, emails, or contact information

✗

We do not monitor or record voice channels

05

Data Storage & Security

All gameplay data is stored in a SQLite database hosted on a secure server. The database contains only the data described in Section 02 — nothing more.

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect stored data from unauthorized access, including access controls, regular backups, and keeping the bot's token and database credentials private and secure.

Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as long as you are a member of a server where Kita is present. You may request deletion at any time — see Section 08.

⚠ Note: As an independent developer project, Kita does not guarantee enterprise-level security. While we take reasonable precautions, we encourage you not to treat any Discord bot as a secure vault for sensitive personal information.

06

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any outside parties. Your data is never shared with third-party advertisers, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

The only third-party platform involved in Kita's operation is Discord itself, which operates under its own Privacy Policy. By using Discord, you are already subject to their terms.

We may disclose data if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

07

Privileged Intents

Kita is approved for the following Discord Privileged Gateway Intents. Below is a plain-language explanation of exactly how each is used:

MESSAGE CONTENT

Purpose: To read the content of messages and detect prefix-based commands directed at Kita (e.g., !pull, !trade). The bot reads message content transiently in memory, extracts the command and any arguments, executes the appropriate action, and immediately discards the content. It is never stored, logged, or analyzed beyond this single-use command parsing.

SERVER MEMBERS

Purpose: To access the member list of servers where Kita is installed. This is used to: (1) resolve user mentions in trading and gifting commands, (2) generate accurate server leaderboards, and (3) validate that trade targets are current members of the server. No member data is stored beyond User IDs, which are already collected as described in Section 02.

08

Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of all data associated with your Discord User ID at any time. Upon a verified request, we will permanently remove your card collection, inventory, economy balance, pity counters, and all other associated gameplay data from our database.

To request a data wipe, you can:

💬

Join the Support Server

Join the official Kita support server on Discord and open a ticket or message a staff member requesting a data deletion. We will verify your identity and process the request promptly.

📩

Contact the Developer Directly

You may contact the developer directly via Discord or the contact methods listed in Section 11. Please include your Discord User ID in your request.

Note: Data deletion is irreversible. Your card collection, coins, and progress cannot be recovered after deletion is processed. Deletion requests are typically fulfilled within 72 hours.

09

Minors

Kita is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13 years of age, or older depending on your country of residence). By using Kita, you confirm that you meet the applicable minimum age requirement set by Discord's Terms of Service.

We do not knowingly collect data from users who do not meet these age requirements. If you believe a user under the applicable minimum age is using Kita and providing us with data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Kita's features evolve or as legal requirements change. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and announce changes in our official support server.

Continued use of Kita after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
